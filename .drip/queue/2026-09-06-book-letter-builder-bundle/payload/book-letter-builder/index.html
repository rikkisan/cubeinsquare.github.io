Where this helps

  • Letters, summons, witness notes, and quest documents that need to feel like real objects.
  • Contracts, ritual instructions, and books players should keep and revisit.
  • Readable flavor text that belongs in the inventory instead of in a long tellraw chain.

Before export

  • If the item should look special, pair it with CustomModelData and a pack-backed icon.
  • Use shorter pages for letters and longer page stacks for journals or contracts.
  • If the book is meant to be signed lore, keep the author field intentional instead of filler.
