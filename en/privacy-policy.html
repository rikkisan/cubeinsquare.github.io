Privacy Policy

This page explains what data Universal MC Pack may process, how cookies are used, and how third-party advertising through Google AdSense works.

Last updated: April 15, 2026.

1. Data processed by the site

Universal MC Pack is a browser tool for creating Minecraft resource packs. Texture files you upload to the generator are processed locally in your browser to build a ZIP archive. The site does not require registration and does not ask for your name, address, phone number, or similar personal information.

2. Cookies and local storage

Cookies are small files that websites or third-party services can store in your browser. They may be used for site functionality, remembering settings, measuring ad performance, and showing more relevant ads.

3. Google AdSense and third-party ads

The site may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to show ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and comply with advertising policies.

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in advertising

4. User files

PNG textures selected in the generator are used locally in your browser. The site does not upload those files to a Universal MC Pack server.

5. Changes

This policy may be updated when site features, advertising services, or legal requirements change. The current version is always published on this page.
