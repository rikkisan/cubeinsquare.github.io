Privacy Policy

This page explains what data Cube in a Square may process, how cookies are used, and how third-party advertising through Google AdSense works.

Last updated: April 15, 2026.

In short

Local processing

The main generators use your PNG files and settings directly in the browser. Under normal tool usage, the site does not upload those files to its own server.

Third-party services

The site may rely on Google AdSense, CDN libraries, and public Minecraft data sources. Those services can receive technical request details from your browser.

Privacy contact

If you have a question about privacy, site operations, or advertising, you can contact us on Discord: kvaasssssssss.

1. Data processed by the site

Cube in a Square works as a browser-based toolkit for Minecraft resource packs and related server workflows. Texture files uploaded to the generator are processed locally in your browser to build a ZIP archive. The site does not require registration and does not ask for your name, address, phone number, or similar direct personal details.

When you visit the site, technical information may be processed by your browser, hosting, and third-party services, such as IP address, browser type, language, approximate network location, visit time, and opened pages. This information is generally used for delivery, security, hosting diagnostics, and advertising.

2. Cookies and local storage

Cookies are small files that websites or third-party services can store in your browser. They may be used for site functionality, remembering settings, measuring ad performance, and showing more relevant ads.

You can disable or clear cookies in your browser settings. If cookies are disabled, some parts of the site or advertising experience may behave differently.

3. Google AdSense and third-party ads

The site may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to show ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and comply with advertising policies.

Google may use advertising cookies to serve ads based on visits to this and other websites. Third-party vendors and ad networks may also use cookies to display ads on this site.

You can opt out of Google's personalized ads in Google Ads Settings and control cookies through your browser settings.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in advertising

4. External resources

To run the interface and Minecraft gallery, the site may load data or libraries from third-party sources, including JavaScript CDNs and public repositories with Minecraft-related data. When those requests happen, your browser may send technical request details to those services.

The site may also link to external websites. We do not control their privacy policies, so it is a good idea to review their own terms separately.

5. User files

Textures selected in the generator are used for local resource-pack creation in the browser. The site does not upload those PNG files to a Cube in a Square server. The final ZIP is created on your device and downloaded through the browser.

6. Children and younger users

The site is about Minecraft resource packs and may be interesting to teenagers. We do not intentionally collect children's personal data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child shared personal information through the site, contact the site owner so the issue can be reviewed and handled if such data was actually received.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated when site features, advertising services, or legal requirements change. The current version is always published on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy, advertising, or site-operation questions, you can contact us on Discord: kvaasssssssss. Basic project information is also available on the Project page.
